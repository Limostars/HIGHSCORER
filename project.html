Beam design projects (Eurocode)

Designing a Concrete Beam according to Eurocode This involves checking that the section complies with the requirements for fire resistance, checking that cover and concrete quality comply with durability requirements, calculate bending moments and shear forces and calculate reinforcement required for bending and shear.

Footing and Foundation design (Eurocode)

The project design of footings to Eurocode involved checking that the ground has sufficient bearing resistance to withstand vertical actions, sufficient sliding resistance to withstand horizontal and inclined actions and sufficient stiffness to prevent unacceptable settlement.
